My Career Path

Dec 23, 2022 by Efim

I decided to write a small post about my career path. By a fork in the road, I mean the choice of where to move professionally next.

Due to layoffs at the start-up company where I worked, I lost my job. I was an integration engineer and was involved in the implementation and support of partners in our meta search engine, in short.

The question now is where to go next.

I have always loved and love learning. Like a drug addict, I buy IT courses and go through each of them, throwing maybe 10 percent. In a word, I'm a nerd. I learn a lot and know a lot.

I have all the necessary stack hard skills to work in the data field: Data Analyst, Data Scientist and Data Engineer. Unfortunately, I have not yet been able to find a job in this area (still trying).

I am also a good web designer. Again, I have all the necessary knowledge and skills. At the moment I am making out a portfolio for myself, I think it will be useful in any case. If the portfolio is ready at the time of this writing, I will attach the link.

I am writing this post simply so that the reader always considers several options when looking for a job, several directions, areas of activity. It's always good to look at what you're doing with a fresh eye. And I donâ€™t see anything wrong with taking a break for a few months, just to understand what you are really interested in, and not stupidly look for a job in an area in which you have experience. I think it's a mistake - don't do it.
