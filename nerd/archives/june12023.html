About Us

Welcome to the BecomingNerd community web page. This community was created and supported by self-educated IT talents

What is really important to us:

  • It is very important for us that the material that our talents prepare for readers is accessible to a wide audience.
  • It is important for us that the material we publish helps the community, as well as the reader, develop professionally or at least motivate them to do so.
  • It is important for us to keep in touch with the reader. That is why we are always glad to have a new audience on our subreddit.
  • It is important for us to help the author of the articles to prove himself. Each of our authors brings to the blog something of his own, his style, his theme, himself. We are for promoting each author, his works and talents.

On our web page you can find articles on a huge variety of topics in different areas of IT: from web development to time management, from programming lessons to motivational articles, from career solutions to creating your own online business. Our talents are very versatile, hence the variety of topics.

If you are interested in joining our community, we will only be happy. We are all very different, because it is so interesting with us.
