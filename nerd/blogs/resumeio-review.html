What?

Resume.io is an online resume builder that aims to simplify the process of creating a professional and polished resume. In this review, we will take an in-depth look at the features and functionality of Resume.io and help you decide whether it is the right tool for you. You can find a huge number of resume generators on the web. In general, it seems to me that every week there is a new one, probably simply because this is a very relevant niche.

Now everyone is looking for a way to optimize and save their time and there is nothing wrong with using ready-made templates for the final version of your resume. For example, I always use templates on such generators, because I know that I will spend most of my time talking not about my experience, but choosing a beautiful design for the document.

If you have doubted until this moment whether you need a resume generator, then the answer is as simple as possible - yes, you do. If possible, save your time and effort, use ready-made designs, ready-made fonts and just fill in the empty fields in the template. Don't make things difficult for yourself, besides, the employer doesn't care if you use a template or write and develop everything yourself.

Interface?

First and foremost, the user interface of Resume.io is intuitive and user-friendly. It guides you through the process of creating a resume step-by-step, providing helpful tips and suggestions along the way. You can choose from a wide variety of templates, each of which is professionally designed and customizable. You can change the colors, fonts, and formatting of your resume with ease.

resume.io - Interface

In this case, the developers thought firstly about the user and did everything possible to make this long process faster and easier, for which they have great respect. All your drafts and old versions of resumes will be automatically saved, and you can return to their work at any time. This is especially true when you have gained a new experience or skill since the last time you edited your resume that is worth noting in the document.

resume.io - All My Resumes and Cover Letters

Unfortunately, there are restrictions on downloading and number of resumes in your profile, which directly depend on the level of your subscription. About subscriptions, we will express our opinion a little lower, do not miss this part.

Specific Job Applications?

One of the standout features of Resume.io is the ability to tailor your resume to specific job applications. You can easily switch between different versions of your resume and customize each one to highlight your most relevant skills and experience for the particular job you are applying for.

resume.io - Specific Job Application

Resume.io also includes a number of powerful tools to help you create a standout resume. For example, the tool includes a library of pre-written content that you can use to fill in your resume. This can be particularly helpful if you are struggling with writer's block or need inspiration for how to word your accomplishments and experience.

I think the huge selection of career-based resume templates is a feature of this service. I analyzed analogues and can say that it is rare for any generators to meet such features. And if some generators have such a division of templates by profession, then the choice is very small and even ridiculous. Here it is presented more comprehensively and thoughtfully.

Prices?

On our site, we never comment on prices for certain applications or services, and we won't this time either. Resume.io offers three subscription levels, the prices for which you can see in the screenshot below. Personally, it seems to me that the most popular and cheapest subscription level is enough to enjoy all the features of the resume generator to the fullest.

resume.io - Prices

I think that the other subscription levels are simply meaningless, because in fact you fill out a resume once, in one or two evenings, and the rest of the time you just send it to different vacancies. So, the sense, for example, in an annual subscription is not great. But as always, it's up to you to decide. This is just my opinion.

Formats?

Another great feature of Resume.io is the ability to export your resume in multiple formats, including PDF, Word, and HTML. This makes it easy to share your resume with potential employers in a format that they can easily view and access. However, almost every site that provides resume generation services now has this feature, so we will stop and talk about it in detail.

Canva

I think you've heard of Canva. If not, I will say very briefly that Canva is an online tool for your designs. Here you can design business cards, presentations and resumes by editing ready-made templates and choosing a design among thousands of elements. The tool is very popular among those who do not know and do not know how to work in Photoshop and do not want to hire freelancers.

Canva - CV Templates

Canva has the ability to create your own designs and resumes. It's just as fast and easy as custom made generators. You won't find such a rich variety of templates here, especially templates that focus on a particular specialty, but this is a good free alternative for those who want to focus on the content of their document and not on its design (like I like to do).

Conclusion

So, do you really need a tool like Resume.io to generate a CV? The answer is that it depends on your individual needs and preferences. If you are comfortable creating a resume from scratch and have a good understanding of what employers are looking for, you may not need a tool like Resume.io. However, if you are looking for a quick and easy way to create a professional-looking resume, Resume.io can be a great option.

Resume.io is a powerful and easy-to-use resume builder that can help you create a standout resume tailored to your specific job applications. With its user-friendly interface, customizable templates, and helpful tools, it can be a great option for anyone looking to create a polished and professional resume.
