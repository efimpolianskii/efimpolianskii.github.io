Dead Link Checker

This is the tool that I use myself practically daily, which is why it is on this list. A free online application called Dead Link Checker looks for broken links on your website. It's a clear, easy-to-use tool that's perfect for novices or anybody who needs to quickly scan their website for broken links.

The program offers a thorough report of all broken links discovered and lets you scan up to 2,500 pages. The report contains the broken link's URL, the page it was discovered on, and the kind of problem that was experienced.

DeadLinkChecker - Interface

The ability to export the data to a CSV file makes it simple to share the report with others or import it into other programs, which is one advantage of Dead Link Checker.With the option to check for broken links on a particular page or the full website, the interface is clear and straightforward to use. A premium version of the program is also available, which enables more frequent tests and more thorough reporting.

Dead Link Checker is a trustworthy and user-friendly application for examining broken links on your website. Although, while it might not have as many capabilities as some other tools, it's a wonderful choice for rapidly locating and correcting broken links to enhance both the usability and SEO of your website.

Ahrefs

A broken link checker is one of the services offered by the well-known SEO tool Ahrefs. To enhance user experience and search engine optimization, the tool is made to assist website owners in finding broken links on their pages and fixing them.

Ahrefs - Broken Link Checker

A thorough report of all broken links discovered on the website is provided by the simple-to-use Ahrefs broken link checker. It can spot broken links on both internal and external sites and gives details on their precise position, the page they were discovered on, and the kind of problem that occurred.

Finding broken links that go to other websites is one of Ahrefs broken link checker's unique capabilities. This is helpful for website owners who want to make sure that their links aren't pointing to resources that are no longer available or relevant. It also makes it simple to share the report with others or transfer it into other programs because Ahrefs' broken link checker lets you export the data to a CSV file.

In conclusion, Ahrefs' broken link checker is a strong and trustworthy tool that may assist website owners in finding and fixing broken links on their pages. Although it could cost more than some other broken link detectors, its feature set and the caliber of its reporting make it a worthy investment for individuals who are serious about enhancing the search engine optimization of their website.

Google Search Console

The broken link checker is one of the tools offered by Google Search Console, which enables website owners to find and correct broken links on their sites.

Google Search Console - Broken Links

Easy to use and offering a thorough report of every broken link discovered on the website is the broken link checker in Google Search Console. The program locates broken links on both internal and external sites and offers details on their precise position, the page they were discovered on, and the kind of problem that was experienced.

One advantage of utilizing the broken link checker in Google Search Console is that it is integrated with other Google products, such Google Analytics, making it simple to watch website traffic and determine the effect of broken links on website performance. The ability for website owners to swiftly and easily correct broken links using the tool's UI is another advantage of utilizing Google Search Console's broken link checker. This can enhance the website's user experience and search engine optimization.

The broken link checker in Google Search Console is a trustworthy and user-friendly tool that may assist website owners in finding and fixing broken links on their websites. It is a great choice for individuals wishing to enhance their website's search engine optimization without spending money on pricey SEO solutions because it is a free tool.

W3C Link Checker

The program is user-friendly and offers a thorough report of all broken links discovered on the website. The W3C Link Checker's ability to identify broken links on both internal and external website pages is one of its most noteworthy characteristics. Each broken link is given a thorough report that includes its position and the kind of problem it caused.

W3C Link Checker - Interface

Also, website owners may modify the scan choices in the W3C Link Checker to scan only a portion of their website or omit particular pages from the scan. This might help you save time and make sure the scan is concentrated on the website's most crucial sections.

The W3C Link Checker's inability to offer alternatives for correcting broken links is one drawback. Broken links must be manually updated by website owners, which can take a lot of time for bigger websites with many of broken links.

In conclusion, the W3C Link Checker is a trustworthy and user-friendly tool for locating broken links on a website.
