IREV is an affiliate marketing company that helps businesses to boost their online presence and revenue through their affiliate marketing services. They work with a variety of industries, including health and wellness, beauty, and e-commerce.

IREV offers a range of affiliate marketing services, including affiliate program management, influencer marketing, and affiliate recruitment. They also provide reporting and analysis to help businesses track their performance and make data-driven decisions.

One of the key benefits of working with IREV is their team's experience and expertise in the affiliate marketing industry. They stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices to ensure their clients are getting the most effective strategies for their business.

CJ Affiliate

We thought for a long time with the team what resources are best for you to choose and came to the conclusion that the most effective and useful resources will be blogs of companies whose business mostly consists of affiliate marketing. The fact is that such companies have blogs, among which you can find a lot of useful information about what affiliate marketing is in general and how it works in every detail. Often such logs are written for potential partners and customers of their SaaS, so they are written carefully and in great detail. One such blog is the CJ Affiliate blog.

CJ Affiliate - Blog Page

CJ Affiliate (formerly known as Commission Junction) is one of the largest affiliate marketing networks, connecting advertisers with publishers. It was founded in 1998 and is based in Santa Barbara, California. One of the biggest advantages of CJ Affiliate is the vast network of advertisers and publishers, allowing for a wide range of partnerships and opportunities for affiliates. They offer a user-friendly platform and a range of tools and resources to help affiliates optimize their campaigns and track their performance.

CJ Affiliate offers a variety of commission structures, including pay-per-sale, pay-per-lead, and pay-per-click, giving affiliates flexibility in how they earn commissions. They also offer real-time reporting and analytics to track performance and optimize campaigns.

However, you and I are not investors, and it is not so important for us what the company does, rather, what matters to us is how it presents it to us, because we firstly evaluate the valuable theoretical material presented to us.
