About Us

Young start-up designed by and for students with the passionate desire to learn new foreign language on their own. Grammer skills are only a top of the iceberg, but how about the rest of it: listening and speaking. Knowing all the rules it is hardly possible to drive a car. That is absolutely the same.

Somebody prefer to find a teacher for trainig speaking skills, somebody learns the topics by heart, but there is a group of people, who not only gets pleasure and broaden the mind of talking to native speaker, but also trains pronunciation, gets rid of accent or just tries to combine words into sentences. We are the team who joins native speakers and students. With us, today you can be a teacher, to teach somebody communicate correctly, tomorrow you can be a student, who uses every opportunity to make foreign language native one.

The concept of our work as easy as to tie laces. Before practicing the speaking skills of the learning language, we ask you to be supportive to our community and teach a class for the student, who is learning the language native for you. After each class you unlock the lesson for you. This time you are the student.

Please remember, we are not school at all, we do not sell lessons or courses. We are community, where only in cooperation each of us can reach the own goal.

HOWs

As soon as we get your request for the practice lesson, we start notifying all the native speakers from our database. You will be replied about the speaker we have found for you as soon as possible. Please note: before being a student you are gonna be a teacher as a native speaker.

Via email or via other contacts that have been left to our manager you will get the native speaker contacts, left in turn. As soon as the exchange of contacts happened, you can deal with the date, time and even duration of lesson.

To join the community as easy as to make tea for the lesson. The only thing you have to do is to write our manager about your desire ti join us. Please note: do not share you contacts to the manager before our manager will ask you to do that.

Contacts exchange can happened once, in case of being satisfied about the lessons. Nevertheless, sometimes it is hard to find a common language with a person or to make a deal about the time and date. In this case, the only thing you have to do is to write our manager about it. Please note: do not forget to write the reason of changing the student/teacher. It is extremely important, because of the next point.

In case of rude, inadequate and inappropriate behavior, the student/teacher immediately get ban and never can join our community again. Our team has a judge for these cases. The only thing judge needs is the evidence (screenshots) of inappropriate behavior.

We are community, where we help each other to get necessary foreign language speaking skills. That is why, please, help somebody else to learn your native language and get help from somebody, whose language you learn. We are achieving goals only together.

Again, we are not school. We do not have courses. Our community is based on mutual assistance, not on getting funds. That is why everything here is free to use.
